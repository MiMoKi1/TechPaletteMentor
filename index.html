About us

Founded by artist King, Tech Palette Mentor, is an app founded at the interospection of art process and technology. King is passionate about bridging the gap between traditional art practices and modern technology. The Old Master copied the older master, how can we do that in 2024> This question led to the coding in HTML, CSS, JAVA, SQL, noSQL, and hosting of frontend/backend files to brigh light to the answer.eam of artists and tech enthusiasts has come together to create an innovative web app designed specifically for artists of all levels. With Tech Palette Mentor, we aim to empower creators by providing personalised colour blending suggestions based on their actual paint inventory. Our mission is to inspire creativity, enhance artistic expression, and help artists explore the endless possibilities of colour theory. Join us on this exciting journey to elevate your artistry!
