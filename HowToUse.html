How to Gain Access to Tech Palette Mentor

Once you decide this blending tool is a MUST for you, buy it, artist! Paint with tech and pay at our shop.

Once you buy access on our Stripe checkout, your email address will be added into our database of users.

It can take up to 24 hours for this to happen, we are artists and work in tech and appreciate your patience.

Log in to the web app with the same email address you input into Stripe, and the controls will appear!

Happy painting and drawing, artists!
